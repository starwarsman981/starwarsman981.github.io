Selected Projects

Quantum Dot Dye Laser Capstone (in progress)

Joint chemistry–physics senior capstone under faculty supervision. Designing and constructing a quantum dot dye laser using CdSe nanocrystals as the lasing medium. Exploring syringe-pump controlled synthesis to narrow emission bandwidths and reduce power selectivity across wavelengths. Synthesizing and characterizing quantum dots and investigating optical performance in a laser cavity.

Hydrogel Transfer Printing (REU)

10-week NSF-funded research program. Investigated hydrogel transfer printing for surface-molded systems, focusing on patterning interdigitated arrays. Applied surface patterning techniques to prototype systems and presented results in a poster.

Ag Leader Steering Algorithm

Developed an algorithm for determining vehicle steering angle from plant location data using computational methods in math and computer science. Analyzed large datasets with Python and Excel. Produced both a paper and presentation for the Simpson College Research Symposium.
